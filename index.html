About

The story behind the sound

RMS emerged from the underground of Canada's jungle scene, carving out a reputation for raw, uncompromising breaks and basslines that shake foundations.

With roots in the golden era of 90s jungle but eyes firmly on the future, RMS blends classic amen breaks with modern production techniques to create something entirely unique.

From packed warehouse raves to studio sessions that push sonic boundaries, the mission remains the same: to architect frequencies that move bodies and minds.
